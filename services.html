Learn About Our Services

At Makeup By Marlene, we offer hair and makeup services for every occasion! Whether you are walking down the aisle, celebrating your graduation, modeling for a photoshoot, or going to a Halloween party, we have the perfect look for you. Our goal is to make you feel confident and, because we believe that when you feel good, you do good.

All of our services are customizable to acheive the look you want. Makeup By Marlene also offers trial appointments so you can get to know the artist and make changes to the look beforehand. For your convenience, we also offer traveling services, bringing beaauty right to your door!

A $50 deposit per person is required to book any service.

Bridal

Our bridal makeup services are designed to enhance your natural beauty and give you a timeless, radiant look for your special day. We focus on long-lasting, photo-ready, flawless application. We tailor your look to your style, whether soft and romantic, glamorous, or bold. We strive to make your experience as calm, stress-free and luxurious, which is why we are particular about the products we use and the techniques we deliver for you. This service requires a 3 hour time frame. Please book your appointments with enough time.

We also offer services for your bridesmaids, so your entire bridal party can look cohesive. Each bride will receive a unique look to complement their features and your overall wedding aesthetic. Each bride requires a 1 1/2 hour time frame. Each additional bride will require an $80 fee. Read the table at the bottom of this page to learn more about the prices.

Quinceañera

Our quinceañera makeup services are designed to give you the perfect balance between extravagant and elegance, while maintaining your youthful appearance. This service focuses on photo-ready and long-lasting application to ensure everything stays intact throughout all the surprise dances! Each look is tailored to complement your dress, colors, and theme. This service requires a 2-3 hour time frame.

Social Events

Social events include diverse occasions such as birthday parties, baby showers, graduations photoshoots, holiday parties, wedding guest, or quinceañera guest. Social event makeup is designed to complement your natural features and match your style and the event being attended. This service requires a 1 1/2 hour time frame.

Hairstyling

We offer hairstyles for all of the occasions mentioned above. We customize our styles to best fit your style and vision. We work with hair of all lengths and textures. We specialize in hollywood waves and effortless curls, however up-do's and blowouts are also a favorite. We ensure that your hairstyle matches the outfit you will be wearing, because every detail matters. We do not work with extensions. This service requires a 1-2 hour time frame.
