Meet the Artist

Marlene Castillo is a certified makeup and hairstyling artist with one year of professional experience and a passion for creating art beyond the canvas. She earned her certification at The Makeup School By Faride Lizeth and has worked with diverse clients to create stunning looks for various occasions. She specializes in bridal, quinceañera, and social events but also editorial and Halloween looks. Marlene proudly serves the Prescott Valley, Arizona and surrounding areas as well as Tempe.
